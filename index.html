About This Tender

UNOPS is procuring a software development firm on behalf of Sri Lanka's Ministry of Health (MoH) to design, build, test, deploy and maintain a national Digital Health Platform — specifically a subset called the "thin-slice." ℹ

This is funded by Germany's Federal Government via the Global Fund (HiQi/Debt2Health programme), active until December 2026. ℹ

The estimated total procurement budget is approximately USD 20 million across multiple bundles. ℹ This tender covers Bundle #2 — the five core digital health systems.

The contract is deliverable-based (fixed price), denominated in LKR, with payment in 8 milestone tranches over 30 weeks. ℹ

What Needs to Be Built — 5 Core Systems + 2 Cross-Cutting Modules

Authoritative longitudinal health record for every Sri Lankan citizen. Consolidates clinical encounters, lab results, diagnoses, treatments, referrals, and care plans across all healthcare levels.
